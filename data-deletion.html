1. Your Right to Data Deletion

At TakeCare, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time, for any reason.

Legal Rights:
  • GDPR (Europe): Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • CCPA (California): Right to deletion
  • LGPD (Brazil): Right to data deletion
  • Global Best Practice: We honor deletion requests worldwide

2. How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Follow these steps:

  1. Open the TakeCare app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Scroll to Account Management
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm deletion by entering your phone number
  7. Tap Permanently Delete Account

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@takecare.app with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your registered phone number or email
  • Include: Account username (if applicable)
  • Optional: Reason for deletion (helps us improve)

Response Time: We will confirm within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

Method 3: Support Request

Contact our support team at support@takecare.app for assistance with account deletion.

4. Data Retention (What We Keep)

Legal Requirements: Some data must be retained for legal compliance:

4.1 Retained for Legal Compliance:

  • Transaction Records: If you had any payments (retained for 7 years for tax purposes)
  • Fraud Prevention: Phone number hash (prevents re-registration for abuse)
  • Legal Disputes: Records related to active legal matters

4.2 Anonymized Data:

We may retain anonymized, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you personally, such as:

  • General app usage trends
  • Feature popularity metrics
  • Performance statistics

This data has no personal identifiers and cannot be linked back to you.

5. Deletion Timeline

Deletion Process:

  • Day 0: You request deletion
  • Day 0-1: We confirm receipt of your request
  • Day 1-7: Your account is deactivated (can still be recovered)
  • Day 7-30: Data is permanently deleted from production systems
  • Day 30-90: Data is purged from backup systems
  • Day 90: Deletion complete and irreversible

Grace Period: You have 7 days to cancel the deletion request. After 7 days, deletion cannot be reversed.
